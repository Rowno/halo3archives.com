Terminal Location:

Terminal 6 is located near the end of the seventh level, The Covenant.

Go to the last tower that you have to clear out in the level (the tower that you clear out before you get the Scorpion tank) and head towards the elevator control panel. Instead of activating the control panel, like usual, walk straight past it and you'll see a dark ledge. On this ledge you'll find the terminal.
