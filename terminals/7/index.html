Terminal Location:

Terminal 7 is located near the start of the ninth level, Halo.

First walk through the start of the level as you normally would until you reach the spot where you have to drop down into the area where you battle the first Flood of the level. Instead of dropping down, turn right and you'll see a narrow gap in the cliff that reveals the entrance of a structure. Head into the structure and turn left, you'll see the terminal straight in front of you.
