Terminal Location:

Terminal 5 is located near the middle of the seventh level, The Covenant.

After getting into a Hornet, fly towards the second tower (the one that the Elites have already disabled). Land the Hornet on the second tower and the terminal is in the main hallway of the structure, across from the only door.
