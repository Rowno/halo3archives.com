Select the armor pieces and colors that you want and a picture of your selected armor will automatically be generated, along with how to unlock the separate pieces of armor and a direct link to the picture.

To find out the Development and History of an armor type, select all the armor pieces of that armor type. For armor types that don't use all the armor pieces, select Mark VI as a replacement for those non existent armor pieces.

All of the armor pictures are loaded from Bungie.net.
